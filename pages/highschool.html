    Linganore High School

    Linganore High School in Frederick, Maryland. Typically High School is a time where people start to figure out what they want to be doing, and it was no different for me.

    I would consider myself a relatively involved student, and enjoyed being busy - a trait that I have not learned to shake off just yet. During my time there, I did one horrific season of Indoor Track (learned the hard way that you actually still practice outside everyday) and 4 seasons of lacrosse. Outside of sports, was in a lot of the boring honor society's but had a great time in French Honor Society. Why did I take French instead of Spanish even though Spanish would have been much more helpful? After signing up for Spanish in 7th grade, I wasn't able to take it because of the math class I was enrolled in and also in band. Which leaves me here barely remembering French while everyone around me speaks Spanish.

    I think that about sums things up from my time, but as always happy to share more. Please leave a message below!
